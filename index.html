About

I'm a Global Cloud, AI & Mobile Solutions Architect with 18+ years of leadership experience designing intelligent, scalable, and secure enterprise ecosystems across automotive, manufacturing, retail, logistics, and large enterprise domains in APAC, India, and Europe.

I specialize in integrating Microsoft Azure (the full PaaS/IaaS stack), AI (Azure OpenAI, Cognitive Services, Bot Framework, RAG Pipelines), and Cross-Platform Mobile (Flutter, .NET MAUI, Xamarin) to deliver cloud-native, event-driven applications with secure APIs and fully automated CI/CD pipelines.

My architectural strengths span end-to-end Azure platform design, AI-driven automation frameworks, enterprise mobile architectures with offline-first capabilities, advanced identity & zero-trust security, and cloud economics optimization through FinOps practices — consistently translating complex business goals into high-performing, cost-efficient solutions.

I lead multi-disciplinary engineering teams across regions, defining architecture roadmaps, driving modernization and automation initiatives, establishing technical governance, and championing engineering excellence. Recognized as a Microsoft Azure Content Hero and Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) with a track record as Technical Owner, Principal Consultant, and Cloud Advisor on major transformation programmes.

Outside of work, I run MSDEVBUILD — a technical community blog and YouTube channel covering Microsoft Azure, AI, Flutter, .NET MAUI, and DevOps — with 500+ articles, regular video tutorials, and conference talks shared with developers across the globe.

Since 2007, I've dedicated my free time to mentoring students from underprivileged backgrounds — helping them find their path in technology, build careers, and transform their lives and their families' futures. Not for money. Just for the love of giving back.

❤️ Read My Full Story 🤝 Get Mentorship
My Story

I grew up in a poor family in India. My parents sacrificed everything to send me to school. When I graduated in 2007 from Coimbatore, I struggled to find work — and when I finally did, the pay barely covered food and shelter.

That experience shaped me. I realised that having a job — any job — was a foundation to build on. And I wanted to help other students find that foundation before they lost hope.

Since then, I've supported over 15,000+ students, been featured in newspapers for this work, and watched many of them go on to build successful careers and happy lives. I measure time not in months, but in the number of students I've seen succeed.

"I've dedicated my time to their careers, not money. Witnessing many lives change and families find happiness."
— Suthahar Jegatheesan
A Promise of Hope

If your parents are poor, it doesn't mean you have to be poor too. Start dreaming and working towards achieving the life you've imagined. Achievement is not the end — my sharing will continue as long as I'm here.
